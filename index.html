BambooTracker

YM2608 music tracker

BambooTracker is a cross-platform music tracker for the Yamaha YM2608 (OPNA) sound chip which was used in NEC PC-8801/9801 series computers.

Features

Multi-platform

It is available for Windows, macOS and Linux systems.

Multi-language

Support English, Japanese, French and Polish translations.

Graphical instrument editor

Editing FM sliders and instrument sequential macros with the mouse.

MIDI input

Entry notes from your MIDI device.

Import

It is loadable other tracker/MML's FM / SSG instruments, ADPCM samples.

Export

Export your module as WAV, VGM and S98 files.
